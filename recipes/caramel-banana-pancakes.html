Caramel-Banana Pancakes Recipe

Today we will cook...PANCAKES!
The best medicine for your hunger and your heart.

Ingredients that you need

  • 6 tbsp of butter(Divided!)
  • 25 KRAFT Caramels(My favorite)
  • 2-1/4 cups of milk(Divided as well)
  • 2 cups of flour(Not divided this time)
  • 3 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tbsp of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 bananas(And we go back to dividing)

Let's cook!

  1. Microwave 2 Tbsp. butter, caramels and 1/4 cup milk in small microwaveable bowl on HIGH 2 min. or until caramels are completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring after each minute.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in large bowl; set aside. Microwave remaining butter in medium microwaveable bowl on HIGH 30 sec. or until melted. Add remaining milk and eggs; whisk until blended. Mash 1 banana. Add to egg mixture; mix well. Add to dry ingredients; stir until blended.
  3. Ladle batter onto hot griddle or into hot skillet sprayed with cooking spray, using 1/4 cup batter for each pancake; cook until bubbles form on tops, then turn to brown other sides.
  4. Slice remaining bananas. Serve pancakes topped with sliced bananas and caramel sauce.
